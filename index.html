About the Project

The AVMS Encyclopaedia Veterinariae began as a visionary project by Umar Adamu Danmari, the current Senate President of the AVMS Parliament, during his campaign for Secretary General. His initial goal was to compile a comprehensive database of academic resources for all veterinary students at ABU Zaria, which was personally sponsored and uploaded to the AVMS Google Drive.

Today, this project is evolving. ANKA's campaign team is proudly sponsoring the development of this website, transforming the Google Drive repository into a simplified and easily accessible platform for the entire AVMS community.

This website was professionally designed and developed by a dedicated team. For inquiries about web design and development, you can reach out for your next project!

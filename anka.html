About Anka

Muhammad Anka Bello is a Veterinary Medicine student at Ahmadu Bello University, driven by a deep passion for animal health, scientific research, and community service. His journey as a vet student has strengthened his values of care, dedication, and responsibility qualities he brings into leadership and student advocacy.

Beyond academics, Bello has taken on leadership and executive roles across student associations, social initiatives, and entrepreneurial projects. His vision is clear: to ensure student welfare, academic growth, and inclusive representation for all members of the faculty.
